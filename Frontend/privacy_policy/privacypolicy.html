Privacy Policy

At Plangenow, we value the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our software application, including when you visit our website and engage with our services.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Plangenow, a platform designed to help you track project activities, time spent on tasks, and collaborate with your team members. By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

2. Overview

We collect personal data, such as your name, email address, and activity-related data, to provide a seamless project management experience. This data enables features such as time tracking, task assignments, notifications, and collaborative activities within your team. The following sections detail the types of data we collect and how we use it.

3. Data Collection

We collect personal and project-related information from you when you sign up, log in, and use our platform. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and profile details.
  • Project Data: Information about the projects you create or participate in, including tasks, time spent, and progress.
  • Activity Data: Time logs and descriptions of activities you add or manage, along with associated challenges and solutions.
  • Team and Collaboration Data: The roles, contact information, and activities of your team members for collaboration purposes.
  • Usage Data: Data about how you interact with the platform, such as pages viewed, actions taken, and features used.

4. Use of Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Project and Time Tracking: To help you create, manage, and track projects, tasks, and time spent on activities.
  • Collaboration: To enable team members to collaborate on projects, share updates, and track progress.
  • Notifications: To notify you of project updates, activity status changes, new challenges, or other relevant information.
  • Account Management: To manage your profile, account settings, and preferences.
  • Analysis and Reporting: To generate reports on project progress, time spent, and team performance.

5. User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Access: You can access your personal data and the data related to your activities and projects at any time through your profile.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your personal information, including your email address, name, and project details.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and personal data by contacting our support team.
  • Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes the use of encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits to ensure the safety of your personal and project data.

7. Cookies

Our platform may use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device to improve site functionality, remember your preferences, and analyze site usage. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may impact your experience with certain features on Plangenow.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, data practices, or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised date will be indicated at the bottom. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
